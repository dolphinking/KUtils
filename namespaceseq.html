Detailed Description

Generic sequence operations and structures.

NB: "sequence" here means "container" in the rest of C++. I just chose "sequence" because "seq" in variable names looks better than "ctr".

Split a sequence into clusters based on some distance metric.

An item is added to a cluster if it is within maxDist from all other items in the cluster.

getDist should take two items and return their distance.
