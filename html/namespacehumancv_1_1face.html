Given rectangles containing face and the size of the parent image, store face skin masks in dest of size imSize.

Faces are approximated as ellipses with a small overlapping rectangle to model the neck.

Parameters:
imSizeSize of parent image where faces were found.
roisVector of face rectangles.
destDestination mask to store faces - will be resized to imSize.
